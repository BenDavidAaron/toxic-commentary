Internet Jerk Detector

This is a simple webapp hosting a Neural Network based model to classify user-supplied comments as toxic or not. The neural net was designed and trained using the Keras library on top of Tensorflow. To improve predictions, and save training time, I also used Stanford's GloVe word embeddings trained on Wikipedia. The final model, while simplistic in it's architecture, has an out of sample accuracy of around 96% which is impressive for a toy project.
